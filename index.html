The Science Behind the Game

The Unfolded Protein Response (UPR) is a critical cellular pathway that helps cancer cells adapt to stress. In T-cell Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (T-ALL), UPR activation can be both a survival advantage and a therapeutic vulnerability. My research explores how modulating UPR components (ATF6, PERK, IRE1) impacts T-ALL progression.
