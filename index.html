The **Unfolded Protein Response (UPR)** is a critical cellular pathway that helps cancer cells adapt to stress. In **T-cell Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (T-ALL)**, UPR activation can be both a **survival advantage** and a **therapeutic vulnerability**. My research explores how **modulating UPR components (ATF6, PERK, IRE1)** can impact T-ALL progression. This interactive game lets you **experiment** with different UPR levels to see how they influence T-ALL behavior.
